About B.Tech

Bachelor of Technology degree is a professional engineering undergraduate degree in India.
Bachelor of Technology is also known as Bachelor of Engineering (B.E) in many institutions.
Study curriculum is same for both B.Tech and B.E.The Bachelor of Technology
(B.Tech) is the 4 year full-time undergraduate course leading to the engineering degree.
