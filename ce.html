About Civil Engineering

Civil engineering is a professional engineering discipline that deals with the design,
construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment, including works like
roads, bridges, canals, dams, and buildings.The course also teaches the development of better
infrastructural facilities to sustain an eco-friendly environment
